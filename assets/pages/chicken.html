Development of Embedded System for Chicken Monitoring Using Deep Learning

Project Overview:

This project involved developing an embedded system to monitor chicken activity, reducing the need for human patrolling. I used a Raspberry Pi 4 and a fisheye camera to capture image data from a chicken coop located at Xinhe Livestock Farm, Leadray Co., Yunlin(雲林新禾畜牧場). The captured images were labeled, and a model was trained to detect chickens. After identifying the chickens' locations, I applied a Simple Online and Real-time Tracking (SORT) algorithm to track their movements. This allowed me to quantify their movements. To calculate how dispersed they were I using the Nearest Neighbor Index (NNI).
