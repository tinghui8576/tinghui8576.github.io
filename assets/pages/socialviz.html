Data Visualization: Impact of Conflicts in Ukraine and the Black Sea Region

Project Overview

The project aimed to explore data from the Black Sea region , encompassing events like battles, violent demonstrations, as well as incidents involving fatalities and injuries, in order to highlight the human toll of conflicts in the area. My initial role involved analyzing the data we got and completing preliminary data preprocessing before determining the narrative direction. Through discussions with my teammates, I created animated visualizations authored the primary narrative based on insights derived from these visualizations.

Key Technologies Used
  • Software: Python, Plotly, Streamlit
