Identifying key skills in job markets through Clustering Analysis

Project Overview

Navigating through numerous job advertisements with varying content and formats can be overwhelming. To address this, we developed a framework in this project to analyze and understand the evolving job market through clustering analysis of job descriptions sourced from the LinkedIn platform. My contributions included using similarity-based vector representations and various clustering algorithms, such as K-Means, DBSCAN, and the Louvain Community Detection Algorithm for graph clustering. After clustering, I applied Named Entity Recognition (NER) models pretrained on skills and knowledge from the job descriptions to identify the skills in each cluster and visualized the clusters using word clouds.

Key Technologies Used
  • Software: Python, Transformers, Clustering Algorithm
